Weekend black bear hunt.

This is a weekend public land hunt, where we'll be getting chartered accross the Squamish River.

Our team has spent a few days along the Squamish River and have identified what we think are black bear tracks, from bears feeding on wild berries.

This is a great hunt for anyone in the Greater Vancouver Area. We'll be meeting in Squamish on the Friday, getting across the river and hunting bear.

The media is using stats and surveys that are slanted against hunters and wildlife conservationists. We are looking to collect questionnaire results to help shed light on the motivations of hunters and conservationists.
