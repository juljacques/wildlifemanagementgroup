Goal and vision

Get Outdoors. Get Started.

"These hunts are designed for people who want to get started with hunting, or just want to get out more often to fill up their freezers with protein"

Expectations

Get comfortable with the uncomfortable.

These are multi-day hunts that will typically require camping, hiking and when the opportunity arises, going in for the kill. So expect to have a good time with fellow hunters, but be prepared for bad conditions. These hunts are perfect for first-time hunters, but you're expected to be self reliant if shit hits the fan.

"Do not expect Basecamp Hunts to be easy. These are backcountry, public land hunts that have a low success rate."

Benefits

- No hefty guide fees
- Meet other hunters
- Share tactics and learnings
- New hunters are welcome
- Split harvest with participants
