Goal & vision

Manage Wild Game Through Active Conservation

"These hunts are designed for people who want to get started with hunting, or just want to get out more often to fill up their freezers with protein"

Expectations

Get Out Into the Field This Season.

These are multi-day hunts that will typically require camping, hiking and when the opportunity arises, going in for the kill. So expect to have a good time with fellow hunters, but be prepared for bad conditions. These hunts are perfect for first-time hunters, but you're expected to be self reliant if shit hits the fan.

"Do not expect Basecamp Hunts to be easy. These are backcountry, public land hunts that have a low success rates."

Benefits

- No hefty guide fees
- Meet with other local hunters
- Share tactics and learnings
- New hunters are welcome
- Split harvest with your group

How it works

Sign-up for a hunt and join other hunters on multi-day excursions to help fill up your freezer with clean and ethical meat.

Purposeful hunts

The basecamp community goes on purposeful hunts, with the intent to harvest all of the meat and hide. Basecamp Hunts are designed to be difficult. They are not run professionally by guides, so do not expect to harvest on every hunt.

Register for a hunt

Save your spot and register for a hunt. It's $29 per hunt or you can subscribe to all BC hunts.

Meetup & setup basecamp

Meet up with all participants at the meetup point and setup basecamp.

Hike, hunt and camp

Get out there and go hunting with other local hunters from veterans to newcomers.

Harvest and share

The harvest is shared between group hunt participants. The hide and trophy goes to whoever took the shot.

Fair chase

We subscribe to fair chase hunting practises.

Harvest everything

From hide, to organ meats, we strive to not spare any part of the animal.

Ethical hunting

Low environmental impact hunting, and full animal recovery.

New hunters welcomed

On every trip we try to have a mix of veteran hunters as well as newcomers to help pass on hunting knowledge.
